Crowd Control


Description

A platform to showcase your abilities to engage an audience and influence them towards a direction you want them to go.


Theme

Video submissions will belong to following domains
1. Digital Marketing
2. Cinematography
3. Cybersecurity
4. Dance / Drama
5. Health and Fitness
6. Entrepreneurship
7. Graphic Design
8. Augmented Reality
9. Vector Art
10. Open Domain


Guidelines

1. Participants will be invited to submit videos (1-1:30 minutes) through google forms on one of the given topics, in which they will need to attract people towards their topics.

2. Shortlisted participants will be invited on the live show and given 5 minutes to ‘influence’ the judges, during which they will be scored in predefined judgement categories. The judges will be free to ask questions from the participants.

3. Every participant will get a participation e-certificate. Top 3 contenders will be given Appreciation e-certificates

Guidelines for Video Submission:

The video must be 1 – 1:30 minutes long.
Submissions will be taken via Google form. Link for the Google form is available on the website.
The content in the video should be relevant to your topic.
Entries will be shortlisted on the basis of speaking skills, originality, and context.
We will judge your content and not the quality of your video. Please reduce its size (10mb limit).
Please keep the content decent.


Timings :

29 Nov 2020 - Launch of event
6 Dec 2020 - Last day of video submissions
7 Dec 2020 - Live Show
