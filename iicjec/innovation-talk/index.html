Innovation Talk


Description

This event aims at conducting a conference among students to discuss futuristic, innovative and creative ideas on various themes provided by IIC-JEC.


Topics

1. What you think is the biggest innovation since apple computers
2. Why Indian tech companies don't go global
3. How transportation will be changed in future
4. Focus on Mars or Rebuild Earth
5. Can technological development become carbon neutral in upcoming decade


Guidelines
1. A discussion among participants from various interests on innovation and creativity.
2. 2 hour discussion where 5 topics will be discussed seperately in different slots among all the participants.
3. Present scenario won't be discussed in detail, participant is supposed to give theit opinion regarding future aspescts.
4. No Languages constraints.
5. Audience and participants can attend all slots if they want. Timing will be specified.

*Every participant will get an E-certificate.


Timings :

2 Dec 2020 - Conference will take place
