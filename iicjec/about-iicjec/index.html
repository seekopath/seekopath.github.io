ABOUT IIC-JEC

MHRDs Innovation Cell envisioned the creation of ❛Institution’s Innovation Council (IICs)❜ across selected Higher Education Institutions with an aim to encourage start-up culture amongst students.

IIC JEC has been build with the same concept following the norms and directives of MHRD's Innovation Cell (MIC).

We aim to enhance the culture of innovation and incubation at JEC, leading to successful college startups, promoted and owned by the people of JEC.

The Innovation council conducts activities in themes of Start-up, IPR, Innovation, Entrepreneurship and others. Ultimately working towards inspiring future innovators while creating a vibrant local modernised ecosystem.
