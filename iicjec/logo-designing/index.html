Logo Design


Description

Logo design is a branding and marketing tool that can be used to signify a business.
Design a new self made logo or re-think, innovate or redesign a pre-existing logo of any Indian start-up. Medium of illustration must be digital illustration or any other creative unique way can be used.


Theme

1. Any established and popular Indian Startup can be choosen. For example zomato, myntra, paytm, flipkart.


Guidelines
1. Participant can use a pre-existing logo from Indian start-up companies and redesign, rethink and innovate it into a new logo.
2. Illustration can be a digital illustration, rangoli colours, collage work, mosiac artwork etc.
Winners will be selected on the basis of :

1.Entries will be judged purely on the basis of originality of the piece, creativity and innovation put together in their work.
2. Entries will be accepted in the form of images, PDF and document.

*Every participant will get an E-certificate.


Timings :

30 Nov 2020 - Launch of event
8 Dec 2020 - Last date of submission
