Youth Speaks


Description

Participants are expected to deliver an extemprorary speech on given topics.
Topics will relate to innovation and creativity among youth


Theme

On the spot themes will be provided.


Guidelines

1. Participant will coordinate with the host for the topic.
2. Each participant will have 5 minutes max to speak on the topic.
3. No Language Constraints.


Winners will be selected on the basis of :

1.Communication Skills
2. How Innovative is your suggestion, etc.
*Every participant will get a E-certificate.
